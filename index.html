Space Shooter

Defend your galaxy in this exciting space shooter game. Control your spaceship, shoot enemies, and survive as long as possible!

How to Play:

  • Move your spaceship: ← → arrow keys
  • Shoot: Press SPACE key
  • Avoid enemy ships and destroy them to earn points
  • Game over when an enemy ship reaches the bottom
